If you manage several clients, the easiest way to keep all analytics in a single dashboard is to use one workspace and connect all client LinkedIn accounts to it.

## **1. Invite Your Clients as Amplifiers**

Go to **Settings → Users & Accounts** and copy your **Amplifier invite link.**

Send this link to each client and ask them to join the workspace and connect their LinkedIn account. Once connected, their Amplifier account will appear on the tab: **LinkedIn Accounts.**

<Warning>
  Each Amplifier seat in Scripe costs an additional 20 euros/month.
</Warning>

## **2. Optional: Add Clients as Creators**

If you want your clients to be able to see their analytics as well, then you must add them to the workspace after they connect their LinkedIn account.

1. Go to **Settings → Users & Accounts**
2. Click **Invite → Invite a teammate**
3. Add your client's email address
4. Under Personal Brands, select the Amplifier account they connected
5. Change their role to **Creator**
6. Send Invite

This ensures the client only has access to their own analytics.

## **What Your Clients See**

When a client logs in and opens Scripe, they will only see:

- Their own LinkedIn account connected
- Their own performance data in Analytics

## **Important**

Whenever you add a new client, always double-check:

1. The user role is set to **Creator**
2. The correct **Amplifier account** is assigned to them

This ensures clients only see their own data while you retain visibility across all accounts.
