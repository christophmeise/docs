### Manage your Notifications

Click Email and/or Slack to access and customize which alerts you’d like to receive from your Scripe workspace:

**General Notifications include:**

- **Post review assigned:** Get notified when you're asked to review a post.
- **Post review completed:** Get notified when someone completes a review you requested.
- **Post comments:** Get notified when someone leaves a comment on a post.
- **Campaign comments:** Get notified when someone comments on a campaign you created or mentions you (Business only).
- **Campaign started:** Get notified when a campaign you are part of is activated (Business only).
- **Engagement requests (Slack only):** Get notified when someone requests engagement on a post.

**Automated emails include:**

- **Weekly post ideas:** Receive an email when new post ideas are generated for you.
- **LinkedIn connection alerts:** Receive alerts when your LinkedIn connection in Scripe needs to be refreshed.

Each option can be toggled on or off depending on your preferences.
