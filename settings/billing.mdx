You can easily manage your subscription, payment methods, billing information, and invoices directly from your Scripe account settings.

## Upgrade or Downgrade

- **Upgrade your plan** depending on your needs.
- **Switch to yearly billing**: automatically save 20% by paying annually instead of monthly. Your subscription will update immediately, and the new billing cycle will apply from your next payment.

<Info>
  Downgrades between plans aren’t available. To switch to a lower tier, simply cancel your existing subscription and re-subscribe to the plan you prefer.
</Info>

## Manage Payment Methods & Billing Details

Under **Billing Details**, you can view and manage the billing address and all payment methods linked to your account.

If you have more than one payment method, you can set one as the **default**.

<Note>
  Updates on billing address will only apply to **new invoices**.
</Note>

## Access and Download Past Invoices

- Under **Invoice History**, you’ll find a list of all your past invoices.
- Click on the invoice you want to download.
- A new page will open where you can click **Download Invoice** to save it.
