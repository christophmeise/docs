Scripe offers three main plans: **Solo**, **Advanced**, and **Business**. 

The right choice depends on how many LinkedIn accounts you manage and the level of strategy and collaboration you need.

<Info>
  [Understand pricing here](https://scripe.io/pricing), or [learn how billing works here](/administration/billing).
</Info>

## Scripe Trial

You can try Scripe for **free.** You are given **7 days** to test out our features before making a decision to purchase a plan.

## Solo Plan

The Solo plan is built for individuals who want to start showing up consistently on LinkedIn and need a simple system to make content creation easier, faster, and more sustainable.

It includes:

- **1x LinkedIn account:** the number of personal LinkedIn profiles you can connect to Scripe.
- **1x User:** the person who logs into Scripe with their own credentials.
- **1x Workspace:** the main environment where your content, analytics, and settings are organized.

And all the following features:

✔️ personalized content strategy\
✔️ personalized tone of voice & knowledge base\
✔️ scheduling and people tagging\
✔️ post preview and formatting\
✔️ kanban post management\
✔️ content interview\
✔️ guided content creation\
✔️ asset generation\
✔️ full LinkedIn analytics (historical data included)

\+ access to **Weekly masterclasses** inside the Scripe Academy

## Advanced Plan

The Advanced plan is made for small teams that create, review, and publish LinkedIn content together.

You can connect **unlimited LinkedIn accounts and users** within a **single workspace**.

<Info>
  The first LinkedIn account connected is €99/month (€79/month on the yearly plan). Additional LinkedIn accounts cost €45/month.
</Info>

Advanced includes everything in Solo, plus:

✔️ auto-engagement across LinkedIn accounts \
✔️ company page management \
✔️ shared content calendar \
✔️ approval workflows\
✔️ team analytics dashboard\
✔️ workspace-wide knowledge base 

\+ access to **Weekly content accountability** calls inside the Scripe Academy

## Business Plan

The Business plan is built for consultants, agencies, and companies managing multiple LinkedIn accounts at scale.

With unlimited users and workspaces under one single bill, it simplifies client or multi-brand management.

<Info>
  The first LinkedIn account connected is €149/month (€119/month on the yearly plan). Additional LinkedIn accounts cost €45/month, and additional Amplifier seats cost €20/month.
</Info>

Business includes everything in Advanced, plus:

✔️ multiple workspaces\
✔️ workspace-wide tone of voice\
✔️ roles and permissions\
✔️ link tracking for revenue attribution\
✔️ custom labels and analytics\
✔️ custom publishing workflows\
✔️ run content campaigns\
✔️ add extra auto-engagement accounts (Amplifier seats for engagement & analytics only)

**\+ 1:1 onboarding** with our team
