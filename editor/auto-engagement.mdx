The Auto Engagement feature helps boost your post’s visibility by automatically scheduling likes, comments, and reshares from your connected team accounts.

<Note>
  This feature is only available on the Business Plan.
</Note>

<Frame>
  ![Auto Eng 3](/images/auto-eng-3.jpg)
</Frame>

## How to Set Up Auto Engagement

Open one of your posts in Scripe and scroll down to the **Auto Engagement** section.

You have two ways to add engagements:

1. Click **Generate Engagements** and Scripe will automatically create a set of suggested likes and AI-generated comments from available team accounts. You can review and edit everything before saving.

<Frame>
  ![Auto Eng 1](/images/auto-eng-1.jpg)
</Frame>

2. Click the **\+ button** next to Auto Engagements to manually add likes, comments and reshares.

You can choose:

- which profile should engage
- the type of engagement
- when it should happen
- the exact comment text (for comments)

<Info>
  Generated comments can always be edited manually before publishing. You can also regenerate individual comments if you want a different variation.
</Info>

## Who Can Be Used for Auto Engagements?

Only accounts that meet both conditions below can be selected for auto-engagement:

1. The LinkedIn account must be connected to Scripe
2. Engagements must be enabled in that Personal Brand's settings

You can manage this under: Settings → [Personal Brand](/settings/personalbrand) → Engagement Settings

## Engagement Settings Explained

Each Personal Brand can control whether other workspace members are allowed to use their account for:

- Likes
- Comments
- Reshares

By default:

- **Likes** → ON
- **Comments** → ON
- **Reshares** → REQUEST

When Reshares are set to **Request**, the user will need to approve the reshare before it gets published.

You can also completely disable engagements for your account at any time from the same settings page.

## AI Auto-Comments

Inside the Engagement Settings page, users can also add example comments they’ve written before.

These examples help Scripe generate comments that better match their:

- tone of voice
- writing style
- personality

The AI also takes the Personal Brand’s Tone of Voice settings into account when generating comments automatically.
