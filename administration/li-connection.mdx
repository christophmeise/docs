Scripe is currently one of the only LinkedIn content platforms that is an **Official LinkedIn API Partner** — meaning your account connection is handled through LinkedIn’s official infrastructure, making it significantly more stable, secure, and future-proof than browser-extension-based tools.

## How to Connect Your LinkedIn

In case you have not connected your LinkedIn during onboarding, you can connect it anytime in two simple ways:

### 1. From Your Dashboard

- On your Personal Brand dashboard, click **Connect** in the top right corner
- This will prompt you to connect your LinkedIn account
- Once connected, the badge will state **Active**

<Frame>
  ![New Li Connection](/images/new-li-connection.jpg)
</Frame>

### 2. From the Personal Brand Settings

- Go to the Workspace Settings
- Select the **Personal Brand** you want to connect
- Click **Disconnect**/**Connect** 
- This will prompt you to connect your LinkedIn account
