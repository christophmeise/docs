Scripe lets you easily switch between **Light**, **Dark**, or **System** themes based on your preference.

<Frame>
  ![Themes New](/images/themes-new.jpg)
</Frame>

## How to Change Your Theme

1. Go to the bottom-left corner of your dashboard screen
2. Click the **“?” icon** to open the help menu
3. Click on **“Theme”**
4. Choose between:
   - 🌞 **Light**
   - 🌚 **Dark**
   - 🖥️ **System** (follows your device’s theme)

Your new theme will apply instantly!
