A **Workspace** is your shared environment in Scripe, where you can collaborate with others to manage multiple personal brands (LinkedIn accounts), coordinate content, and streamline LinkedIn activity across your organization.

<Info>
  If you're on the **Solo Plan**, you'll only see one Personal Brand in your Workspace.
</Info>

<Frame>
  ![New Workspace 1](/images/new-workspace-1.jpg)
</Frame>

## What is a Workspace?

This is where all admin settings, shared resources, analytics, and team coordination live.

Each workspace:

- Can host **one personal brand** on the Solo Plan and **unlimited personal brands** (aka LinkedIn accounts connected) on the Advanced and Business Plans.
- Can have **one user** on the Solo Plan and **unlimited users** helping manage these accounts on the Advanced and Business Plans.

<Info>
  **You don't pay per user.** That means you can invite collaborators freely without worrying about extra costs.
</Info>

## What lives inside a Workspace?

Once inside a workspace, you’ll find access to:

- **Inbox:** see all your Scripe notifications organized in one place, from campaign updates to comments and product updates. Notifications are User-Specific & Personal-Brand-Specific. [Learn more →](/sidebar/inbox)

- **LinkedIn Accounts:** All Personal Brands inside your workspace, including Company Pages.
  - You can add new Accounts by clicking on `+` next to LinkedIn accounts.
  - You can see all the Accounts connected by clicking on `More`.

- [Calendar](/workspace/team-calendar): plan content across multiple brands in one shared view
- [Campaigns](/workspace/campaigns): plan campaigns and track them across all or selected personal brands.
- [Knowledge Base](/workspace/team-knowledge): set writing rules and context for all brands.
- [Analytics](/workspace/team-analytics): track team performance, post results, and top-performing content
- **Settings:** manage [members & roles](/settings/people), [billing](/settings/billing), [post statuses](/settings/post-status), integrations, etc.

<Tip>
  Everything that powers content creation, including uploading materials, drafting posts, and storing knowledge, lives **inside each** **personal brand**.
</Tip>

## How to Switch Workspaces 🔄

If you're on the Business Plan, you can have unlimited workspaces. To switch between workspaces:

1. Click on your **workspace name** in the top-left corner of Scripe.
2. Select **"Switch workspace."**
3. A menu will appear showing all the workspaces you belong to.
4. Click on the workspace you want to enter or add a new workspace.
