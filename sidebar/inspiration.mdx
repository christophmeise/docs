The **Inspiration** page in Scripe is your personal feed of high-performing LinkedIn content.

<Frame>
  ![4 Inspo Page](/images/4-inspo-page.jpg)
</Frame>

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Follow Creators" icon="bell">
    When you follow a creator by clicking the bell icon, Scripe will continue showing their content in your Inspiration Feed. You can unfollow them anytime to stop seeing their content there.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Repurpose Content" icon="sparkles">
    Click the sparkly icon on any post to instantly repurpose it into a post draft you can customize. 
  </Card>

  <Card title="Search on LinkedIn" icon="magnifying-glass">
    A powerful search bar that lets you find _anything_ on LinkedIn. Type in any word, phrase, or even a profile name and we’ll instantly show you the most relevant, high-performing posts.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Filter & Sort the feed" icon="sort">
    Filter by language, industry, engagement, content types, and post formats. Sort between Newest, Relevance or Engagement to find content that fits your research goals.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
