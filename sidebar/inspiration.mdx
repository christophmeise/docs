The **Inspiration** page in Scripe is your personal feed of high-performing LinkedIn content.

![New Inspo](/images/new-inspo.jpg)

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Follow Creators" icon="bell">
    When you follow a creator by clicking on the bell icon, Scripe will analyze their writing and use it to refine your [**Tone of Voice**](/sidebar/tov). It’s an easy way to mirror the style of top-performing authors you admire.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Repurpose Content" icon="sparkles">
    Click the sparkly icon on any post to instantly turn it into a post draft you can customize. It’s perfect for fast repurposing of content you love.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Search on LinkedIn" icon="magnifying-glass">
    A powerful search bar that lets you find _anything_ on LinkedIn. Type in any word, phrase, or even a profile name and we’ll instantly show you the most relevant, high-performing posts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Filter & Sort the feed" icon="sort">
    Filter by language, industry, engagement, content types, and post formats. Sort between Most Relevant, Most Recent, or Most Engagement to find content that fits your research goals.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
