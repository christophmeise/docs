The Tone of Voice page is where you personalize the writing style for each Personal Brand inside Scripe.

Scripe analyzes your best-performing LinkedIn posts and learns how you naturally communicate — your rhythm, phrasing, sentence structure, and emotional tone. It then adapts your future posts to sound just like you.

<Tip>
  The more you post, the smarter this gets. Scripe constantly updates your voice based on what works best.
</Tip>

You can further customize and fine-tune everything: from your writing length and language to emojis, formatting, and even favorite CTAs.

## What You Can Personalize

### 1. Post Structure

Under Post Structure, you can define how your posts are written.

- **Post Language:** Choose between English 🇺🇸 or German 🇩🇪.
- **Sentence Length:** Adjust how short or detailed your sentences typically are.
- **Post Length:** Control the overall size of generated posts.
- **Emoji Frequency:** Choose how often Scripe should use emojis in your content.

These settings help Scripe match your communication style.

### 2. Style Guidance

In the Custom Instructions, you can give Scripe very specific guidance on how to write for you.

<Danger>
  **Warning:** Changes to these settings may reduce post quality if not used carefully.
</Danger>

Here you can provide detailed instructions about your writing style, tone, and preferences. Include personality traits, patterns to use or avoid, and any specific guidelines.

Examples:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Do's" icon="check">
    🔸 Write with high enthusiasm, vulnerability, and playful confidence.

    🔸 Frequently start posts with a personal anecdote or confession, then pivot to lessons or advice.

    🔸 Use arrows and bullet points to structure lists, often with a conversational lead-in.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Don'ts" icon="do-not-enter">
    - 🔸 Avoid generic descriptions.
    - 🔸 Avoid words like: “Moreover,” “Furthermore,” “In conclusion," etc.
    - 🔸 Don't write in a salesy or overly promotional tone.
    - 🔸 Don't overuse jargon or buzzwords without context.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### 3. Workspace Guidelines

Workspace Guidelines apply across all Personal Brands within a workspace. They are managed through Workspace Settings and are available exclusively on the Business Plan.

This feature is especially useful for teams and agencies that want to maintain consistent messaging, terminology, formatting, and brand communication standards across multiple accounts.

### 4. Signature Elements

Teach Scripe the small details that make your writing style recognizable.

- **Frequently Used Emojis:** Add emojis you commonly use in your posts.
- **Frequently Used List Symbols:** Add your preferred formatting symbols and bullet styles.
- **Common CTAs:** Add the call-to-actions you frequently use in your posts.
- **Custom Footer:** Add a reusable signature or footer that can automatically appear in all of your posts.

### 5. Reset if You Need a Fresh Start

If you ever want to start over, use **Reset Tone of Voice**.

This will overwrite your manual settings (custom instructions, emojis, CTAs) and revert back to Scripe’s original AI-analyzed tone.

<Danger>
  This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Please proceed with care.
</Danger>
